## JCR/JIF edition note

IEEE TCSVT, TGRS, TMM, and TIM use the [IEEE Title List, January 2026](https://open.ieee.org/wp-content/uploads/IEEE-Title-List-January-2026.pdf). Its footnote states that JIF and quartile data are based on the **2024 Journal Citation Reports**, released June 2025, and that the list was current on 2026-01-01. Publisher journal pages were used for other displayed JIF values when the metric year was not explicitly visible; those records keep `jifYear: null` rather than treating the 2026 lookup date as a 2026 JIF year.
